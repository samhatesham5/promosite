Kid Cosmic is Netflix orginal animated series following Kid, a young boy living with his grandpa in a junkyard along a highway in Southwest United States, and his dream of becoming a superhero.

Everything changes when a spaceship crashes nearby and Kid finds five "Cosmic Stones of Power" onboard that give him the chance of fulfilling his dream.
